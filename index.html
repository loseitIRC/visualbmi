VisualBMI

About

VisualBMI shows you what weight looks like on a human body. Using a large index of photos of men and women, you can get sense of what people look like at different weights or even the same weight. If you find this website useful, please share it or send a note.

Credits

The data on this website was automatically indexed from subreddits on reddit.com. Huge thanks to all those who share their stories and photos in these communities. This website would not be possible without them. Also thanks to imgur who hosts the images.
